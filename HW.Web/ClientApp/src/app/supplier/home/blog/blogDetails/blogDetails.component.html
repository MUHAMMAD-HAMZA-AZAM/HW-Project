Step 1: Create a job – it’s free

Duration: less than 1 min

At this stage we'll ask you questions about your job such as:

  • where you want the job doing,
  • what type of tradesperson are you looking to hire?
  • your contact details. Relevant trades cannot get in touch with you without these details.
  • a short description of the job.
  • where you want the job doing,

The information you provide here helps us match up relevant trades in your local area. It also allows the tradespeople to give an accurate estimate when they contact you.

Step 2: Trades get in touch

Duration: up to 24 hours

  • Tradespeople will now begin to quote your job and will contact you, usually by phone, to discuss your job and availability. Once you've been contacted by 2-3 trades you can then compare their estimates and choose whomever you prefer.

Step 3: Homeowner’s select tradesman

  • HoomWork encourage homeowners to invite tradespeople at job place and discuss actual work load, time and price.
  • Homeowners accept bid from received quotes and initiate job.

Step 4: Make payment and leave feedback

After the work is complete, make payment to tradesman, return to the site and rate your tradesperson based on quality of their work, reliability and value for money.
