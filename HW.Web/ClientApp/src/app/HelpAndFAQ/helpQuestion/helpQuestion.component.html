What’s the purpose of an FAQ page?

To address frequently asked questions about your business, of course.

Actually, that’s only part of it.

An FAQ section done right can be an effective addition to your website that serves several functions, from:

  • Alleviating purchasing anxieties that your product page copy doesn’t directly address.
  • Relieving some of the burden on customer support by publicly answering common questions.
  • Improving SEO and site navigation.
  • Earning trust by demonstrating product expertise and explaining your business model.
  • Delighting customers by creatively answering their questions.

If you're not using your FAQ page to its full potential, here are some of the questions you should be asking to get the most out of this often-forgotten section of most websites.

How do I post a job?

Visit our homepage at HoomWork or mobile APP, select get free quotes and follow steps to post a free job and get quotes.

I don’t know which category to post my job?

If you’re not sure which category to post your job under, take a browse of our category listings here to see which job types are available under each category. If still not sure then select “others” category and fill the form to post a job. our tradesman will contact you to discuss further.

Will you publish my contact details when I post a job?

HoomWork won’t reveal your full address until a bid is accepted by you. To keep the market competitive and protect your personal information, a maximum of 3 tradespeople can view your name & Town name. So, all you have to do is post your job and the tradespeople will come to you.

We require an address for all jobs as we want to connect you with a tradesperson who is local to you. This means the job is completed in a timely manner and a fair price for you.

What happens after I post my job?

After you post your job, we’ll forward your job description to all relevant tradespeople in your area. Those who are interested in completing your job will contact details and contact you to quote. To keep the market competitive and protect your personal information, only a maximum of 3 tradespeople can view your name and town of the job.

You’ll also be able to use the free call feature within the app, where you can call the tradesperson.

What is best practice to engage tradesman?
  • Post job at HoomWork with full job description
  • Interested tradesman will respond you with their initial Bid
  • Select tradesman and invite to visit job place. Invite minimum 3 tradesman to visit job place
  • Confirm price, time and payment mode
  • Ask tradesman to resubmit bid with actual price
  • Select and confirm bid
  • fter job finish leave feedback and make payment to tradesman
Why do you ask for a budget?

Your budget helps us estimate the size of the job. Setting an approximate, but realistic, a budget helps ensure that the right tradespeople contact you to quote.

I have had no response from my job post. Why is this?

There could be several reasons why you have not had a response to your job. The main reasons are typical:

  • Lack of detail about the job
  • You are in an area we don't currently have a tradesman who can do the work
  • Your budget might not be realistic to get the type of work completed
Do job postings expire?

Yes. Jobs are only live in our system for 7 working days. We also take job postings down if 3 tradespeople have quoted them.

Can I cancel my job advertisement?

Yes. You can cancel your job posting at any time and selecting ‘My quotes’ from the main navigation. Click the job you don’t wish to advertise any longer and then click the ‘cancel job’ button.

How do I pay to tradesman?

We encourage to decide payment method directly with tradesman as HoomWork is not responsible for payment terms.

What happens if I’m not happy with a tradesperson’s work?

In the vast majority of cases, our homeowners have a positive experience using our service, but we know that there may be occasions when things don’t work out. HoomWork doesn’t involve directly to resolve an issue rather provide guidelines and initiate communication between the homeowner & tradesman.

Should I rate my tradesperson?

Yes. After the work is completed, we ask that you provide a rating on the quality, value and reliability of your chosen tradesperson. Your rating and feedback is a valuable tool for other users of HoomWork when evaluating their own tradespeople. Also, positive ratings are essential in helping good tradespeople build their business and secure future work.

What is marketplace?

An online marketplace is a type of e-commerce site where product or service information is provided by multiple third parties. HoomWork marketplace is targeted shopping place from building materials to homeware items.

How do I pay for the item?

HoomWork doesn’t provide shopping basket at the moment. User simply select the product and contact supplier to arrange method of payment and mode of delivery.

Do you give any warranty of products?

No! HoomWork doesn’t own products. These ads are from third parties’ suppliers. Thus, hoomwork unable to provide any warranty.
