It shall be against our policies to do anything that manipulates the search or browse experience on Hoomwork. The user must be sure to learn about our guidelines on See our Guidelines before publishing a listing or advertisement

Brand names:

Allowed:
  • A brand name can be mentioned in a listing, as long as the item is authentically branded with that brand or product name.
  • A celebrity name can be included in a listing, as long as the celebrity created, officially endorsed, owned, or signed the item. Celebrities can also be mentioned if they are depicted in the item for sales, such as on posters or photos.
  • Multiple brand names can be used, as long as there are items by each of those brands in the listing. Various designer names can only be specified if more than one item made by those designers are to be sold in a single listing.

Not Allowed:
  • Brand names, celebrities, or other product details cannot be specified in a listing if you are not selling an item by those brands.
  • Comparing the item, you're selling to a popular product.
  • Misspelling brand names to try to circumvent the rules.
  • Using a question mark '?' when you're not sure about the brand.
  • Providing details such as MPN or UPC that don't match the item you're listing.
  • Using inaccurate or misleading information for required product details (such as "Does not apply" or "N/A") when describing required product identifiers (such as brand, UPC, or MPN) or information other than what the manufacturer provided if the manufacturer has provided that information about your product.
Categories:

Allowed:
  • Select a category that matches the item for sale.
  • Select an applicable secondary category
  • Select the Other category when there isn't one that exactly matches your item
Not Allowed:
  • Selecting a category that doesn't match the item in order to include details for a different product isn't allowed
  • Selecting a secondary category that doesn't match the item isn't allowed
  • Selecting a similar category that doesn't match the item.
Item details:

Allowed:

Provision of clear and accurate details about an item that helps people make intelligent buying decisions - and enables them to make reasonable expectations upon receiving a product.

Not Allowed:
  • Selection of product details from a catalog provided by the company that do not match the items being listed.
  • Selecting an item that does not match the user’s item description, such as 'New' item condition for an item that has been used.
  • Provision of details such as MPN or UPC that do not match the item listed.
  • Contradicting your item condition in item specifics with pictures or your item description.
  • Including terms in the wrong field of your listing, such as including postage terms in the Condition description field.
Pictures & Videos

Including pictures & videos in the listings helps buyers decide whether to purchase your item. Always provide clear and detailed pictures that truly represent your item so there is no confusion about what you are selling.

Allowed:
  • Photos and video, you created yourself
  • You can add maximum 5 photos and 1 video.
Not Allowed:
  • Mages or text copied from websites or internet searches.
  • Images and text copied from listings of other HoomWork members.
  • Scans or text copies from catalogues or advertisements.
