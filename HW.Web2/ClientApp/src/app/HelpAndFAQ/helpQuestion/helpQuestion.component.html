What’s the purpose of an FAQ page?

To address frequently asked questions about your business, of course.

Actually, that’s only part of it.

An FAQ section done right can be an effective addition to your website that serves several functions, from:

  • Alleviating purchasing anxieties that your product page copy doesn’t directly address.
  • Relieving some of the burden on customer support by publicly answering common questions.
  • Improving SEO and site navigation.
  • Earning trust by demonstrating product expertise and explaining your business model.
  • Delighting customers by creatively answering their questions.

If you're not using your FAQ page to its full potential, here are some of the questions you should be asking to get the most out of this often-forgotten section of most websites.

How do I post a task?

Go to the Hoomwork site HoomWork or download the mobile app. Select ‘post a task’ and post a task for free.

In which category to post my task?

Hoomwork offers multiple categories and below every category is a list of task types. Select your preference or go to others, type your task nature and our professionals will contact you to talk over further.

Can tradesmen view my contact details when creating a task post?

Hoomwork reveals contact name and town details only to the first three openings/highest bidders.

However, the exact location and contact details will expose to a tradesman who has accepted the bid.

What happens after I post my task?

Hoomwork shares your task details with similar local tradespeople. The interested ones request a bid. Note, only three tradesmen can contact at a time.

You can also click ‘free call’ to communicate with the tradesman.

How to best reach tradesman?
  • Post a task for free.
  • Write complete description.
  • Interested tradesmen will respond by the first Bid.
  • After you accept the bid, you can invite tradesmen to visit. You can send an invitation only to 3 tradesmen at a time.
  • Decide price, time and payment mode.
  • Select and confirm a bid.
  • Make payment and leave feedback after task is finished.
Why do you ask for a budget?
  • Saves from headache and un-necessary meetings.
  • Help gain trust .
  • Help measure performance and determine the size of the task .
  • Ensure that the correct type of tradesperson contact you with a quote.
No one is responding on my Task. What should I do?
  • Double-check details.
  • Elaborate more details.
  • Your area doesn’t have any local tradesman, try the nearest location.
  • Come up with a realistic budget.
Does the task on Hoomwork expire?

Yes, task posting expires after 15 working days. Same way, they are taken down if the request for bids gets matched with up to 3 local tradespeople.

Can I cancel my task advertisement?

Yes. You can cancel your task posting at any time and selecting ‘My task’ from the main navigation. Click the job you don’t wish to advertise any longer and then click the ‘cancel ’ button.

How do I pay to tradesman?

We encourage you to choose a mutually approved payment method, as we are not responsible for payment terms.

What to do if I’m not happy about work done by a tradesperson?

When a tradesperson doesn’t do the job properly. Hoomwork allows you to rate a person negatively. Moreover, to best resolve the issue homework act as a mediator and try to initiate a dialogue between the homeowner & tradesman.

Why should I rate a tradesperson?
  • It helps evaluate the quality, value, and reliability of the chosen tradesperson.
  • Your rating and feedback can help others evaluate the registered tradesman.
  • Top rating will contribute to building Hoomwork credibility & security.
  • Negative remarks help evaluate tradesman performance.
What does Hoomwork Online Marketplace offer?

Hoomwork online shop offers a variety of homeware shopping items. Ranging from kitchen musthaves to homeware selection, construction and maintenance materials.

How to pay for an item on Hoomwork?

Hoomwork is not offering a shopping basket facility at the moment. Both, user and supplier can mutually decide the method of payment and mode of delivery by contacting each other.

As the ads are uploaded by third party suppliers and Hoomwork does not guarantee or warranty with third party supplier products.
