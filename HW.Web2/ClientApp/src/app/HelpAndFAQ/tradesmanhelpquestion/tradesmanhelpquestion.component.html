What is a task lead?

Task leads is a request that a customer task on the Hoomwork website. We sort requests via address, trade and skill type to pass on details to tradespeople.

How to view and bid task leads?

You can view and bid task leads in a variety of ways:

  • On the main of a website: Upon logging in to your account, you can find the list of latest leads. You can view more details by clicking ‘read more details’
  • On our mobile website: On the app landing page, view the complete task leads list. To see details click read more link.
  • Via email alerts: turn on email alerts and receive notifications of the latest leads.
  • With our mobile app: On the app landing page, view the task lead list. Tap view for detailed information.
How can I control which task leads I receive?

Control task leads by tailoring categories and areas you would like i.e. your address, activity area and trade/skills at the time of creating the account. Tap ‘edit profile settings’ to change settings at any time

Why is my task lead list not nurturing many leads?

Hoomwork creates task leads based on address, activity area, and trade/skill settings. Having these values/working area set incorrect might trouble finding similar task leads.

How can I bid a task lead?

How do I edit a bid once submitted?

What happens once I bid a task lead?

After bidding a task lead, the customer accepts the bid. The customer may call a tradesman to further discuss the details. To increase the chances of winning more work, we share your profile page, ratings and contact details. A single customer can review three tradespeople profiles at a time. It is best to bid first to win more work.

What’s the next step after the bid is accepted?

Once the customer fully understands you. He contacts you to visit his place to fully understand effort, price and time. If the task requires more money, over time than tradesman can modify and update bid with an actual amount and resubmit again.

What if the actual project cost is higher than the bid accepted?

The tradesman can always edit the bid and resubmit at a competitive price.

How to contact the customer?

At the time of bidding a task lead, we offer ‘call back request’ alert. That notify the customers to call back within the first minute. However, this facility can be avail through Mobile Application.

Why should I request for rating?

Reviews and ratings help build a solid reputation online. Whenever a customer reviews a tradesman profile at the time of bidding a lead, he reads ratings present on the page. This is why it is important to have positive ratings on the page.

I am a new joiner. How can I win work on Hoomwork if I don’t have ratings?

In such a scenario, the best way to win more clients is through value-based marketing. Simply follow the steps mentioned below.

  • Outline your previous work, qualification, and specialties.
  • Start with low-cost, easy-to-service leads where customers pay less importance to ratings
  • Come in contact with the customer immediately after bidding a task lead.
  • Give a thorough, detailed quote and provide references.
  • Always ask your customers to leave feedback once the task is done.

On what terms you suspend trades people’s accounts?

We are committed to eliminating any risks posed by customers. This is why we close accounts if they were;

  • Not willing to find a fair and reasonable solution for negative feedback or complaints.
  • Receiving negative reviews or serious complaints from a customer in their initial few months
  • Received three instances of poor workmanship within the last 12 months (based on negative feedback or a complaint being raised).
  • After addressing a wider issue or complaint, we reason there is sufficient cause to remove a tradesperson from our service.
  • Caught behaving improperly, including being racist or sexist, threatening, bullying, stealing from or abusing the trust of customers.
  • Receiving serious complaints relating to their work or personal conduct from outside of the Hoomwork site.
  • Not complying with trading standards, government bodies or the Police.
  • Setting up multiple Hoomwork accounts for personal gain.
  • No longer passing identity validation.
  • No longer meeting the financial stability criteria we set.

This detailed information is by no means exhaustive and is there to guard both landowners and truthful tradespeople. So, in case your venture becomes financially challenging and complaining.
