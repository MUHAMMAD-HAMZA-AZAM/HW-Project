What is a marketplace?

An online, e-commerce marketplace is a website or an app that facilitates people to buy and sell things. Hoomwork online store offers multiple merchants the facility to advertise their products/items and increase conversions and enhance visibility.

How does the Hoomwork marketplace works?

Hoomwork connects business customers to sign up, create & publish listings. Customers view listings and contact suppliers to know more about the items. Hoomwork is not offering a shopping basket at the moment. Both suppliers and customers decide the payment method and delivery mode mutually.terms & conditions.

Who can publish listing/Ads at the Hoomwork marketplace?

Hoomwork online marketplace allows access to business customers only.

The hoomwork marketplace is a one window solution for homeowners and users who are seeking to buy homeware items, kitchens must-haves and construction materials and much more. Thus, Hoomwork allows suppliers to promote homewares & building materials at the site.
